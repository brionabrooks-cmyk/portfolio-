Intuit Mailchimp · FY26 Q2

ABM Sales Play #3: Klaviyo Switcher & Digital Sales Paid Social Campaign

Paid Media Jan–Feb 2026
1 The Brief

Mailchimp needed to convert high-value Shopify merchants ($750+ MRR) away from Klaviyo and toward Mailchimp's Digital Sales Business (DSB) tier. The January launch window required a phased creative approach: first optimizing existing ecommerce assets, then delivering Klaviyo switcher-specific ads — all aligned to an updated messaging hierarchy featuring customer testimonials and competitive differentiators.

2 Strategy

I developed a full-funnel paid social creative brief spanning Meta and LinkedIn across Attract and Engage stages. The strategy centered on two message tracks: DSB ecommerce social proof (Shopify integration, automation ROI claims) and Klaviyo switcher conquest messaging ("Break Up With Klaviyo" creative, migration offers, 42% cost savings). LinkedIn ads used personalization macros (%FIRSTNAME%, %COMPANYNAME%) for account-level relevance, while Meta prioritized testimonial-anchored static and carousel formats. Deliverables included 16 DSB assets, 6 Klaviyo switcher assets, and 64+ lines of supporting copy.

3 Outcome

The phased asset delivery enabled Sales Play #3 to launch on schedule on January 14. The creative framework established a repeatable switcher campaign playbook — reusable testimonial-led creative with modular copy — that was carried forward into broader Q3 planning.

22 Total assets delivered
2 Message tracks (DSB + Klaviyo)
Meta + LinkedIn Channels activated
On time 1/14 launch hit

Intuit Mailchimp · Digital Sales

Go-to-Market: Digital Sales Play #2 — Shopify E-Commerce ABM Launch

Go-to-Market Oct–Dec 2025
1 The Brief

Mailchimp needed to win more high-value e-commerce deals ($750+ MRR) among Food & Beverage and Health & Wellness Shopify merchants — a segment where Klaviyo dominated mindshare and Mailchimp historically lacked traction. The play needed to launch within 1.5 weeks of the mobilization workshop and build the capability foundation ahead of a larger Q3 initiative in January.

2 Strategy

I collaborated across marketing and sales to design a full-funnel ABM motion targeting ~88K identified Shopify accounts. The go-to-market included: a segmented paid social plan across Meta, LinkedIn, and DV360 (Attract → Engage → Lead Gen); a webinar series anchored to Shopify integration and product features; personalized LCM nurture sequences; in-product discovery placements within QuickBooks Online; and Sales-aligned outbound sequences (12-touch, 3 weeks) with ICP-specific email templates and call scripts. A 30%-off introductory pricing offer was paired with complimentary migration services to accelerate Klaviyo switchers at the $750+ MRR threshold.

3 Outcome

Play #2 launched October 6, 2025 as planned. The campaign targeted 88K accounts with marketing reach, with Sales prospecting 5–10K and a target of 100–220 SQLs created. Projected MRR from the play was $45–105K, with a $1.5K average deal value. The program also built the ABM muscle and data infrastructure (technographic scoring, tiered SFDC account views, playbook templates) carried into the January Play #3 launch.

88K Accounts reached
100–220 SQLs targeted
$45–105K Projected MRR
6 channels Paid, webinar, nurture, IPD, web, search
My role: GTM strategy, messaging map development, ABM channel planning, cross-functional launch coordination (Marketing, Sales, Analytics)

Intuit Mailchimp · B2B Marketing

DTC 2026: The New Rules of Growth — Expert Webinar Series

Email Marketing Mar 2026
1 The Brief

Mailchimp was largely absent from the ecommerce expert conversation — Klaviyo dominated mindshare with DTC operators. The brief was to reinsert Mailchimp into that conversation by producing a high-credibility, external expert-led event targeting mid-market DTC prospects and customers. Success meant building pipeline, not just awareness: the stretch registration goal was 1,000, with a 50% MQL rate target.

2 Strategy

I helped design and execute a virtual roundtable featuring 7 external DTC operators and investors — including Nik Sharma, Rabah Rahil, and Ari Murray — with a combined 400K+ follower reach and $500M+ in DTC revenue managed. Promotion ran across 3 prospect email sends, 1:1 sales outreach sequences, organic social, panelist networks, and ICP landing pages. The event was designed to generate a durable on-demand asset, seeding into nurture flows, YouTube, and quarterly newsletters post-event.

3 Outcome

The event exceeded its stretch registration goal with 1,210 registrants and 410 live attendees. 1 in 4 live attendees converted to a sales-qualified lead — 118 leads routed to sales (114 net new, 4 re-engaged). 624 paid accounts attended (51% of all registrants), validating the ICP targeting. 264 free-to-paid upgrade targets were added to the nurture pipeline.

118 Leads to sales
23% Attendee-to-SQL conversion
51% Paid accounts in audience
My role: Program strategy, speaker sourcing and management, email promotion planning, post-event lead routing and on-demand distribution
